Tennis Statistics Tracker

Open Web App:

CLICK HERE TO OPEN
Use this web app to record statistics of tennis matches, such as points won, points lost, service points won, service points lost, player formations, double faults, and much more. When the user needs to keep track of statistics categories that are not provided by default (e.g. was the point won at the net or baseline), they can press the "Custom Statistics Group" button when creating a match to create a custom template for recording different statistics. Match data can be saved into a json file, allowing for the sharing of match data.
